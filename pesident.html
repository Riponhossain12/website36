NAZMUL HASSAN, MP

PRESIDENT

Nazmul Hasan Papon is the president of Bangladesh Cricket Board. Papon was born on May 31, 1961. He has two sisters Tania Rahman and Oanima Bakht. His father Zillur Rahman, was the president of Bangladesh . He completed his MBA from Institute of Business Administration, University of Dhaka. He is the first elected president of the board with his predecessors being appointed by the Bangladesh Government. He was elected unanimously. He is a member of parliament from Bangladesh Awami League
